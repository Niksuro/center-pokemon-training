Hello!

And welcome to pokemon center of training and investigation.
You can check the pokemon stats with only click over them!
Or if you want to check the battle, follow the instructions.

  • Inside the header, at the top-right (or just top if you are in mobile), you can change into "Battle Mode" clicking that button, when this state is activated, the background turns black and each click will select your pokemon (You can see the label of ones are selected in the top screen!)
  • Select two pokemons (even if the same two times!), this will be marked with a yellow card.
  • After that, the VS button will be enable, click it and see the magic!
